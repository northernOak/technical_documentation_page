How to Skateboard

Which Foot Forward

Choosing Goofy or Regular stance

Figuring out which foot goes forward is the first step, a good way to find this is to act like you're sliding on ice. Which foot would you put forward?

How to Ride and Turn

Its time to start cruising

Now that you know how to stand, lets try pushing. The best way to start is to simply walk onto your board. Keep your front foot straight, and push with your rear foot while keeping shoulders open.

Your First Ollie

Lets get to jumping

First practice standing on your tail, while sliding your foot up the deck. Then in one motion, pop the tail, slide your foot and jump. Practice them in the grass or on a piece of rug if your having a hard time, then move to rolling ollies.

FS and BS 180

Lets do some spins!

The biggest tip is to get your shoulders into it, and use the momentum to carry you around 180 degrees. Think about using more power and keeping the board underneath you. Its best to practice in a tight space to keep you from falling backwards or forwards.

Practice makes perfect

Start with shuv-its, pop shuv-its and then move onto heelflips and kickflips. This will take time and lots of effort, dont give up and refer to as many different tutorials as possible. The key is to commit and learn to try new things, its all about problem solving.

It's all about having fun!
